Elizabeth Perez and Melanie Hoff

This interview is narrated by Elizabeth Perez and Melanie Hoff and took place on January 21, 2020 at School for Poetic Computation, 155 Bank St, New York, NY 10014 in response to Cybernetics of Race/ism and Sex/ism by neta bomani and Melanie Hoff. Elizabeth and Melanie discuss the following keywords: motherhood, birth, doula, milk, reproduction, children. Transcription by Shea Fitzpatrick and neta bomani.
